Interactive Flashcards

Click on a card to flip and reveal the meaning.

Hello

Wave your hand near your head.

Thank You

Place your fingers on your chin and move your hand forward.

Yes

Nod your fist up and down like a head nod.

No

Tap your index and middle finger on your thumb.

Help

Place your hand on your palm and raise it slightly upwards.

Stop

place your non-dominant hand flat with the palm facing up, and then bring your dominant hand down forcefully to tap its surface, striking it as if using an axe.

Start

Hold your non-dominant hand flat with fingers spread. Then, insert your dominant hand's index finger between the index and middle fingers of your non-dominant hand and twist it

You're welcome

Touch your index finger to your chin, then move your hand in a slight curve away from your face to touch your chest, palm facing inward.
